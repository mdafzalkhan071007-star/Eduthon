Why Mental Health Matters

The Importance of Mental Health

Mental health is a fundamental aspect of our overall well-being, just as important as physical health. It affects how we think, feel, and act, influencing our ability to handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Good mental health enables us to realize our potential, cope with life's challenges, and contribute meaningfully to our communities.

Unfortunately, mental health issues are increasingly common, especially among young people. According to the World Health Organization, one in four people will experience mental health issues at some point in their lives. For students, the pressures of academic performance, social expectations, and future uncertainties can significantly impact mental well-being.
